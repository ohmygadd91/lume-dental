Holistic Design

A Bespoke Clinical Journey

A smile makeover is a comprehensive treatment plan that addresses multiple aesthetic and functional concerns simultaneously. Rather than treating individual issues in isolation, we assess your smile as a complete composition — considering proportion, symmetry, gum architecture, and facial harmony.

Your plan may incorporate veneers, whitening, bonding, gum contouring, orthodontic alignment, and implant restoration — sequenced precisely to achieve a result that looks naturally yours, not clinically manufactured.

The Process

Five Phases to Your New Smile

01

Facial & Smile Analysis

Comprehensive photography, 3D scanning, and facial proportion analysis. We study your lip line, gum display, tooth-to-face ratio, and midline.

02

Digital Smile Design

Using DSD software, we create a photorealistic simulation of your new smile overlaid onto your facial photographs. You approve the design before any clinical work begins.

03

Phased Treatment Plan

We sequence every procedure — whitening before veneers, orthodontics before bonding — to ensure each step builds on the last with architectural precision.

04

Clinical Execution

Each procedure is scheduled with optimal recovery intervals. We coordinate across specialists — prosthodontist, periodontist, orthodontist — as a single cohesive team.

05

Reveal & Maintain

Reveal & Maintain

Your final appointment is a reveal session. We photograph your results, provide a tailored maintenance protocol, and schedule follow-ups at 3, 6, and 12 months.
