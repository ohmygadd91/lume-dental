The Science

What Is Professional Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening uses concentrated hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide gels activated by precision LED light. Unlike over-the-counter strips, clinical-grade formulations penetrate enamel uniformly, breaking down chromophore molecules responsible for discoloration.

At Lume Dental, we calibrate concentration and exposure time to your enamel's unique sensitivity profile, ensuring maximum brightness with zero compromise on tooth integrity.

The Process

Four Steps to Brilliance

01

Comprehensive Assessment

Digital shade analysis and oral health screening to establish your baseline and identify any sensitivity considerations.

02

Custom Formulation

We prepare a peroxide gel calibrated to your enamel type — balancing maximum efficacy with comfort thresholds.

03

Precision Application

Clinical-grade gel is applied under controlled LED activation. Each cycle lasts 15–20 minutes, with up to three cycles per session.

04

Aftercare Protocol

Take-home maintenance trays with a lower-concentration gel to preserve your results for months. Dietary guidance included.
